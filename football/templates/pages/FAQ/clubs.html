Clubs FAQ

How do I join a club?

Follow the following steps:

  • Head back to the home page by clicking on the logo at the top.
  • At the home section scroll down just above the footer, you will see our listed clubs.
  • Clicked on the specific club you would wish to join.
  • Scroll at the bottom of the club page to the footer.
  • Under "More Info" click "Contact us" and fill in the form and submit.

Wait for a response from the team.

Can I create my own club?

Yes, you can create your own club and register interest to take part in Ligi Open. After creating your club:

  • Head back to the home page.
  • Click "Register Club" under the welcome message.
  • You can also scroll to the bottom of the page on the footer.
  • Under "More" click "Register Club".
  • Fill in the club details and submit.

You can additionally Click here to register your club's interest.
